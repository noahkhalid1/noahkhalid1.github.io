Midnight Express Diner

First lets start with the menu. It's sooo good! You can basically order breakfast at any time in the day. Why not get chocolate pancakes at 12am?

Lets start with their burgers. It doesnt matter what type of burger you order, it will be good. And the fries will be just what you need to go with it.

The breakfast menu is top tier. THERE IS A WHOLE PAGE JUST FOR BREAKFAST OPTIONS. WOW. It ranges from eggs and omlettes all the way to bagels and muffins.

AND the Pancakes...Oh MY God. So good. Whatever type of pancake you like, they can provide it to you. Chocolate chip? Yes. Chocolate Banana? yes. Chocolate, banana, strawbarry, nutella, with whip cream? YES.

Lets talk about the milkshakes. Strawberry, Chocolate, Vanilla, Oreo, and...Brownie!? Even though brownie sounds disgusting, apparently its not. All the other ones taste and sound great. Who doesn't want to have a milkshake at 11pm on a saturday?
